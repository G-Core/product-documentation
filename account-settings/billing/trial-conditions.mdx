## Overview

The trial period allows you to test the service for free for a limited time, regardless of the plan you choose. Not all services offer a trial period. The table below shows which services offer trials, and any related conditions or comments.

To find information on specific services and tariffs, check [Gcore Pricing](https://gcore.com/pricing/edge-network) or the Gcore Customer Portal.

**Service** | **Trial** | **Conditions**  
---|---|---  
CDN (Free) | - | The free plan has no trial period but has a traffic limit 1 TB  
CDN (Start, Pro) | + | 14 days or 300 GB, whichever ends first  
Edge Cloud (AI, Basic VM, DDoS Protection) | - | -  
Video Streaming | + | 14 days and 10 minutes of uploaded video  
Web Application Security | + | 3 days  
Managed DNS (Free) | - | The free plan has no trial period or limitations  
Managed DNS (Pro) | + | 14 days  
Object Storage | - | -

## How to start a trial

You can activate the trial for any of the plans for which it is available.

_If you are a new user_, the tariff activation page will appear when you open your [personal account](https://accounts.gcore.com/reports/dashboard). Choose the service that has a trial. Click **Next** and follow the plan activation flow instructions.


<Frame>![Starting page where you can choose a service to start](https://assets.gcore.pro/docs/account-settings/billing/trial/trial-10.png)</Frame>



_If you already have some services activated_, tariff plan activation (and trial if it exists) will be on the [Services](https://accounts.gcore.com/billing/services) Billing page. Click **Activate service** and follow the instructions. 


<Frame>![Services page where you can also start a trial](https://assets.gcore.pro/docs/account-settings/billing/trial/trial-20.png)</Frame>



You can also activate the plan by choosing the relevant service in the left navigation menu.

That's it! We will send you an email at the beginning of the trial and two days before the end of the trial.

Contact support ([support@gcore.com](mailto:support@gcore.com) or chat) or your manager to extend the trial duration or add GB.

<Info>
**Info**

The flow differs for some services (e.g., DDoS Protection). Follow the recommendations from the Gcore Customer Portal to activate the service.
