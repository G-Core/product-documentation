A trial period allows testing a Gcore service for free for a limited time, regardless of the plan chosen. For pricing details on specific services and tariffs, check [Gcore Pricing](https://gcore.com/pricing/edge-network) or the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://portal.gcore.com).

## Trial availability by service

Not every service offers a trial. The following table shows which services offer trials, and any related conditions.

| Service | Trial | Conditions |
|---------|-------|------------|
| CDN (Free) | Not available | Free plan has no trial period, but has a 1 TB traffic limit |
| CDN (Start, Pro) | Available | 14 days or 300 GB, whichever ends first |
| Cloud (AI, Basic VM, DDoS Protection) | Not available | Not available |
| Video Streaming | Available | 14 days and 30 minutes of video |
| Web Application and API Protection (WAAP) | Available | 30 days |
| Managed DNS (Free) | Not available | Free plan has no trial period or limitations |
| Managed DNS (Pro) | Available | 14 days |
| Object Storage | Not available | Not available |

Customized trial conditions are available for Enterprise plans. Contact support or an account manager to discuss them.

## Starting a trial

A trial can be activated for any plan that offers one.

For new accounts, selecting a service with a trial from the left navigation menu opens a welcome page for that service. Click **Get started for free** to begin the trial; no credit card is required.

<Frame>![Welcome page for a service with a Get started for free button](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/trial-conditions/trial-10.png)</Frame>

For accounts with services already activated, tariff plan activation (including any available trial) happens on the [Services](https://portal.gcore.com/accounts/settings/services) page, under **Account** in the Customer Portal. Click **Activate service** next to the required service and follow the on-screen instructions. A plan can also be activated by selecting the relevant service from the left navigation menu.

<Frame>![Services page listing services with Activate service buttons](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/trial-conditions/trial-20.png)</Frame>

An email confirms the start of the trial, followed by a reminder two days before it ends. Contact support ([support@gcore.com](mailto:support@gcore.com) or chat) or an account manager to extend the trial duration or add gigabytes.

<Info>
The activation flow differs for some services, including DDoS Protection. Follow the instructions in the Customer Portal to activate these services.
</Info>
