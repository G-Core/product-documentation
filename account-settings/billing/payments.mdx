Payment for Gcore products follows either a pay-as-you-go model, based on actual resource consumption, or a fixed monthly cost tied to a committed plan. Costs for each product are listed on the [Pricing page](https://gcore.com/pricing).

## Payment history

To view payment history:

1. In the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://portal.gcore.com), click the profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Profile**.
3. Navigate to **Billing** > **Payments**.

The Payments page lists every payment operation processed for the account.

| Column | Description |
|--------|--------------|
| ID | Unique identifier of the transaction |
| Created | Date when the payment operation was created in the system |
| Paid | Date when the payment was successfully completed |
| Payment method | Method used for payment (Credit or debit card, PayPal, bank, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Alipay) |
| Sum | Total paid amount |
| Status | Current processing state of the transaction |

<Frame>
  ![Payments page in the account settings](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/payments-page.png)
</Frame>

### Payment statuses

The Status column shows one of five processing states.

| Status | Description |
|--------|--------------|
| Paid | Payment completed successfully |
| Unpaid | Payment failed |
| Cancelled | Payment was cancelled |
| Pending | Payment is being processed |
| Refunded | Payment has been refunded |

### Payment data filters

Payments can be filtered by payment method, date, or status using the relevant table dropdowns.

## Payment methods

Payment methods are added, verified, and removed on the **Payment methods** page. To open it:

1. Click the profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Profile**.
3. Navigate to **Account** > **Payment methods**.

<Frame>
  ![Payment methods page in the account settings](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/payment-methods-page.png)
</Frame>

### New payment method

A payment method is added directly on the **Payment methods** page.

1. Select the payment system to use (Credit or debit card, PayPal, Google Pay, or Apple Pay). The form expands.

2. Enter the payment information.

<Info>
The payment method address is separate from the [billing address](/account-settings/account/overview), so changes made on one page do not affect the other.
</Info>

3. Click **Confirm information** to apply the changes.

### Payment method verification

Adding a payment card triggers a brief verification step to confirm it's valid before it can be used for charges.

#### Verification charge

A verification charge of approximately 1 EUR/USD applies when a payment card is added. This is not a fee, it's a temporary authorization hold confirming that the card is valid.

#### Refund

Refunds are processed automatically, usually instantly or within 24 hours, though it can take up to 3–7 business days depending on the bank.

#### Multiple charges

Each attempt to add a card creates a separate hold, and all holds are refunded automatically.

### Default payment method

The default payment method is the option automatically selected for transactions unless a different one is chosen. Multiple payment methods can be added; the first one added becomes the default, though it can be changed at any time by clicking the three-dot icon next to the preferred method and selecting **Make default**.

<Frame>
  ![Example of a card with Make default option available](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/make-payment-method-default.png)
</Frame>

### Payment method removal

A payment method cannot be deleted if it's the only one on the account.

1. On the **Payment methods** page, locate the card to remove.

2. Click the three-dot icon on the card and select **Delete**.

3. Click **Delete** again to confirm.

## Balance top-ups

Beyond automatic billing, funds can be added to the Gcore account balance manually or through automatic top-ups.

### Manual top-up

A manual top-up adds funds to the Gcore account balance using any available payment method.

<Note>
Full payment information may be requested during a manual top-up in some cases.
</Note>

A one-time payment with Alipay is available for manual top-ups, without adding it as a saved payment method.

To top up the balance with Alipay:

1. In the Customer Portal, click **Top-up** in the upper-right corner.

<Frame>
  ![Top-up button](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/top-up.png)
</Frame>

2. In the **Recharge now?** window, open the **Payment method** list. If a payment method is already added, select it for the top-up, like **Card - Mc 0010**, or select **Pay once with Alipay**.

<Frame>
  ![Recharge now window payment method](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/recharge-now-alipay.png)
</Frame>

3. Select a preset amount or enter a custom amount in the **Charge sum** field.

4. Click **Recharge** and complete the payment.

<Frame>
  ![Recharge now window payment method](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/recharge-now.png)
</Frame>

Once the payment completes, the funds are added to the account balance.

### Auto top-up

Auto top-up automatically replenishes the Gcore account balance when it falls below a specified amount.

| Setting | Description |
|---------|--------------|
| When balance falls below | Threshold amount that triggers an automatic top-up |
| Top up balance to | Target amount reached after the top-up |
| Payment method | Payment method used for automatic charging |

<Info>
Only active cards and digital wallet payment methods can be used for auto top-up; bank payment methods are not supported.
</Info>

Auto top-up is configured on the **Payment methods** page.

<Frame>
  ![Set up auto top-up](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/payment-methods-auto-top-up-disabled.png)
</Frame>

It can also be enabled by clicking **Top-up** in the top-right corner and selecting **Set up auto top-up**.

Once auto top-up is set up, Gcore charges the selected payment method automatically whenever the balance falls below the threshold.

#### Auto top-up statuses

The auto top-up configuration shows one of three statuses.

| Status | Description |
|--------|--------------|
| Active | Auto top-up is enabled and working normally |
| Disabled | Auto top-up is configured but disabled |
| Suspended | Auto top-up was temporarily disabled because multiple payment attempts failed |

Updating or verifying the payment method reactivates a suspended auto top-up.

#### Suggested values

Suggested values, generated automatically from recent account usage, offer an alternative to manual entry.

The suggestions include:

- Recommended threshold amount
- Recommended target amount
- Average daily spend
- Maximum daily spend during the analysis period

<Frame>
  ![Auto top-up suggested values](/images/docs/account-settings/billing/payments/auto-pop-up-suggested-values.png)
</Frame>

Minimum recommended values are suggested instead when no usage history is available.
