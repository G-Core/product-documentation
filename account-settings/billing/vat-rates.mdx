Value added tax (VAT) is a consumption tax assessed on the value added to goods and services. The VAT rate charged on a Gcore invoice depends on the customer's user type (organization or individual) and country.

For an organization, providing a valid EU VAT number reduces the applicable rate to 0% (reverse charge) in most EU member states. Without a validated VAT number, the standard rate for the organization's country applies instead. For an individual, the rate depends only on the country of registration; individuals outside the EU are charged 0%.
