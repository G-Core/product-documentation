To change the password for your account: 

1\. In the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://accounts.gcore.com/reports/dashboard), navigate to the account settings in the top-right corner of the screen, and click **Profile**.


<Frame>![Profile settings in the Customer Portal](/images/docs/account-settings/my-profile/change-password/dashboard-account-dialog.png)</Frame>



2\. Open the **Change password** page.


<Frame>![Change password page](/images/docs/account-settings/my-profile/change-password/change-password-page.png)</Frame>



3\. Enter the current password once and the new password twice.

<Frame>![Change password dialog](/images/docs/account-settings/my-profile/change-password/change-password-dialog.png)</Frame>

4\. Click **Save** to apply the updates.

The password has been changed. You will receive an email notification informing you about the password change.


<Frame>![Email confirmation of a password change](/images/docs/account-settings/my-profile/change-password/password-change-confirmation.png)</Frame>



## Reset a password

If you forgot your password, you can easily reset it on the [reset password page](https://auth.gcore.com/login/forgot-password). Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. 


<Frame>![Password reset page](/images/docs/account-settings/my-profile/change-password/password-reset-page.png)</Frame>
