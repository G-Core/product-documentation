## Deletion overview

You can request account deletion at any time. Once initiated, your account enters a Suspended state and all services stop functioning immediately.

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to back up any data you need before requesting deletion.
</Warning>

After the deletion is processed, your account, data, and services will be permanently and unrecoverably deleted. You will receive a notification confirming the deletion.

<Note>
If your account has a debt, it won't be deleted until the debt is paid off.
</Note>

## How to delete your account

Go to **Settings** > **Account** > **General information** and scroll down to the **Delete account** section. Click **Delete my account**.


<Frame>![How to delete your account](/images/docs/account-settings/account/account-deletion/delete-account-button.png)</Frame>



In the pop-up that appears, please complete the short survey and click **Confirm**.


<Frame>![Deletion survey dialog](/images/docs/account-settings/account/account-deletion/deletion-survey.png)</Frame>



Once you've submitted a deletion request, all of your services will be suspended and will stop functioning. Your account and any services you've created will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

## When can an account NOT be deleted?

If your account has a debt (negative balance) and you send a deletion request, it will be declined. You will receive an email notification explaining the reason. In such an instance, settle the debt and then submit a new deletion request.

To check whether your account has a debt or not, open the [Billing](/account-settings/billing/overview) section and check the top right corner of the page.
