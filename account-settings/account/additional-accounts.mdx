A single Gcore login controls multiple accounts without re-authorization. Switching between them is useful for anyone managing several accounts at once.

## Create an additional account

To create an additional account:

1\. Click the profile icon in the top-right corner of the page.

2\. Click **Switch account** to expand the account menu.

3\. Click **Create a new account**.


<Frame>![Create a new account option in the profile menu](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/create-new-account-menu.png)</Frame>



4\. In the **Create an additional account** dialog, enter an account name and click **Create account**.


<Frame>![Create an additional account dialog](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/create-account-dialog.png)</Frame>



<Note>
The account name must be unique. If the name already exists in the system, the account will not be created.
</Note>

After the account is created, the Dashboard for the new account opens automatically.


<Frame>![Dashboard after account creation](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/dashboard-after-creation.png)</Frame>



The services of a newly created account are inactive by default.

## Switch to another account

To switch to a different account:

1\. Click the profile icon in the top-right corner of the page.

2\. Click **Switch account** to display a list of all available accounts.


<Frame>![Switch account menu with account list](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/switch-account-list.png)</Frame>



3\. Click the desired account to switch to it.

## Log in to a specific account

To log in directly to one of several linked accounts:

1\. Enter a username and password on the [authorization&nbsp;page](https://auth.gcore.com/login/signin).

2\. If multiple accounts are linked to the login, a selection page appears for selecting the account to use.

3\. Click the required account to log in.
