title: Create an additional account and switch between accounts
sidebarTitle: Additional accounts
---

You can manage multiple accounts from a single login without re-authorization. This is convenient if you are a user of several accounts and need to switch between them while working.

## Create an additional account

1\. Click on the profile icon in the top-right corner of the page.

2\. Click **Switch account** to expand the account menu.

3\. Click **Create a new account**.


<Frame>![Create a new account option in the profile menu](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/create-new-account-menu.png)</Frame>



4\. In the **Create a new account** dialog, enter an account name and click **Create new account**.


<Frame>![Create a new account dialog](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/create-account-dialog.png)</Frame>



<Note>
The account name must be unique. If the name already exists in the system, the account will not be created.
</Note>

After the account is created, you will be automatically redirected to the Dashboard of the new account.


<Frame>![Dashboard after account creation](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/dashboard-after-creation.png)</Frame>



The services of a newly created account are inactive by default.

## Switch to another account

1\. Click on the profile icon in the top-right corner of the page.

2\. Click **Switch account**. A list of all accounts available to you will appear.


<Frame>![Switch account menu with account list](/images/docs/account-settings/account/additional-accounts/switch-account-list.png)</Frame>



3\. Select the desired account and click on it. You will be redirected to the selected account.

## Log in to a specific account

1\. Enter your username and password on the [authorization page](https://auth.gcore.com/login/signin).

2\. If you have access to multiple accounts, you will be redirected to a page where you can select the account you need.

3\. Click on the required account to log in.
