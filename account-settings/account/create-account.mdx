Creating a Gcore account grants access to the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://portal.gcore.com) and every Gcore service, including Cloud, CDN, and Managed DNS. Individuals can sign up directly with an email address, Google, or GitHub, while legal entities that need a signed contract can complete the same sign-up process and contact sales separately.

## For individuals

1\. To sign up for Gcore services, go to [the Gcore website](https://gcore.com) and click **Sign up for free** in the top navigation.


<Frame>![Gcore homepage with the Sign up for free button highlighted](/images/docs/account-settings/account/create-account/get-started-gcore-website-10.png)</Frame>



2\. On the sign-up page, enter an email address and password, then click **Start for free**. Alternatively, click **Google** or **GitHub** to create the account with an existing account from either provider.


<Frame>![Gcore sign-up page with email, password, Google, and GitHub options](/images/docs/account-settings/account/create-account/create-gcore-account-20.png)</Frame>



3\. When signing up with email, a verification link is sent to the specified email address. Click the link to confirm the email. If the email doesn't appear in the inbox, check the spam folder or click **resend the confirmation email**.


<Frame>![Confirm email page with the resend confirmation email option](/images/docs/account-settings/account/create-account/confirm-email-30.png)</Frame>



4\. After confirming the email, the Gcore dashboard becomes available in the Customer Portal.


<Frame>![Gcore Customer Portal dashboard after first login](/images/docs/account-settings/account/create-account/gcore-dashboard-40.png)</Frame>



5\. Select a product from the sidebar and start creating a resource. Every product requires activation before it unlocks full access, and the exact prompt depends on the product: in Cloud, creating a virtual instance shows a banner to click **Activate the product**.


<Frame>![Activate the product banner on the Create Virtual Instance page](/images/docs/account-settings/account/create-account/activate-cloud-product-50.png)</Frame>



6\. Select a payment method, either **Credit or debit card**, **PayPal**, **Google Pay**, or **Apple Pay**, then enter the billing address.


<Frame>![Billing information page with payment method options](/images/docs/account-settings/account/create-account/check-billing-info-60.png)</Frame>



## For legal entities

Entities that don't need a contract can complete the individual sign-up process described above to start using Gcore services.

Entities that need a contract can reach Gcore via chat or email at [support@gcore.com](mailto:support@gcore.com), and the sales department will follow up. The sign-up process can still be completed to gain access to the services while the contract is being prepared.
