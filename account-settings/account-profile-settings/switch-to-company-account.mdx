Individual Gcore accounts can be converted to company accounts through a verification process. Company accounts provide additional billing features and allow business information to appear on invoices.

## Individual vs. company accounts

| Feature | Individual | Company |
|---------|------------|---------|
| Account type label | "individual" | "company" |
| Business details on invoices | No | Yes |
| VAT/Tax ID support | Limited | Full |
| Business ID field | No | Yes |
| Billing contact options | Basic | Extended |

## Prerequisites

Before starting the switch request, prepare the following information:

- Legal company name (as registered with authorities)
- Business registration number (Business ID)
- VAT or Tax ID (if applicable)
- Legal business address
- Contact phone number (optional)

## Submit a switch request

To request conversion from an individual to a company account:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Account** > **General information**.
2. Click **Switch to company account** in the top-right area of the General section.
3. Complete the form fields:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Your name | Yes | Full name of the person submitting the request |
| Your email | Prefilled | Account email address (read-only) |
| Company name | Yes | Legal registered company name |
| Website | No | Company website URL |
| Phone number | No | Contact phone number |
| Business ID | Yes | Business registration number from the relevant authority |
| VAT/Tax ID | No | Value Added Tax or Tax Identification Number |
| Street | Yes | Legal address street |
| City | Yes | Legal address city |
| ZIP/Postal code | Yes | Legal address postal code |
| Country | Yes | Legal address country |

4. Click **Submit request**.

The request enters a review queue. Processing time varies based on verification requirements.

## Request status

After submission, the request status can be tracked through email notifications sent to the account contact address. Possible statuses include:

- **Pending**: Request submitted and awaiting review
- **In review**: Verification in progress
- **Approved**: Account converted to company type
- **Rejected**: Additional information required or request denied

If a request is rejected, the notification email includes the reason and instructions for resubmission.

## After approval

Once the switch is approved:

- The Account type field changes from "individual" to "company"
- Business details become available for editing
- Invoices include company information
- The **Switch to company account** option is no longer displayed

<Info>
Switching to a company account is a one-way operation. Company accounts cannot be converted back to individual accounts through the portal.
</Info>

## Contact support

For questions about the verification process or to check request status, contact Gcore support through the chat widget in the Customer Portal or via email.
