Detailed plan information, feature comparison, and pricing for WAAP are available on the [Security pricing page](https://gcore.com/pricing/security#waap).

## Trial

Each paid plan includes a **30-day trial period**. During the trial, you can test all features included in the selected plan.

## Plan availability

WAAP plans are available as either self-serve or sales-assisted offerings.

- **Free**, **Start**, and **Pro** plans are self-serve and can be activated directly through the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://accounts.gcore.com/reports/dashboard).

To change a subscription plan:

1. Navigate to **WAAP** in the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://accounts.gcore.com/reports/dashboard).
2. Select **Change plan**.

<Frame>
  ![Change plan](/images/docs/waap/getting-started/change-plan.png)
</Frame>

- The **Enterprise** plan includes the full WAAP feature set, combining all capabilities from the Start and Pro plans with additional advanced features and flexible quotas. It is available through direct engagement with [Gcore Sales](mailto:sales@gcore.com) and supports negotiated contracts, enterprise agreements, and tailored commercial terms.

Organizations requiring procurement support, contractual agreements, or enterprise-specific capabilities should select the **Enterprise** plan.
