WAAP Analytics helps security teams, platform engineers, and DevSecOps practitioners understand how protected applications and APIs behave under normal and hostile traffic conditions. It provides a security-focused operational view across protected domains, combining high-level visibility with request-level investigation.

Analytics data is available in UTC. After WAAP protection is enabled for a CDN resource, it can take up to 20 minutes for telemetry and analytics to populate with live data.

WAAP Analytics is organized into three sections:

- **[Dashboard](/waap/analytics/dashboard.mdx)** — Monitor security posture and traffic patterns across domains.  
- **[Events](/waap/analytics/events.mdx)** — Investigate requests, attacks, and mitigation actions in detail.  
- **[Domains](/waap/analytics/domains.mdx)** — Understand protection coverage across assets and start domain-focused analysis.
