---
title: "Analytics"
---

To access WAAP analytics:

1. In the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://accounts.gcore.com/reports/dashboard), navigate to **WAAP** \> **Domains**.

<Frame>
  ![Domains page in the Customer Portal](/images/docs/waap/analytics/waap-domains-blur.png)
</Frame>

2. Find the domain where you want to check analytics and click the domain name to open it, or select **All Domains**. You'll be directed to the **Overview** page.
3. In the sidebar, click **Events**.

<Info>
  **Info**

  After you enable WAAP protection for your CDN resource, it might take up to 20 minutes to populate your domain with real data and apply all system-wide settings.
</Info>
