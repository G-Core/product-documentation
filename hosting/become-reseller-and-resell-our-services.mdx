As a Gcore reseller, you purchase servers and provide them to your customers through your own billing system.

To join the reseller program and receive a reseller discount, [contact&nbsp;support](/hosting/contact-our-technical-support) and provide the following information:

- Company name
- Company website URL
- Number of users
- Product to resell (VPS or Dedicated Server)
- Expected sales volume

Once your request is approved, the discount will appear under **Client** > **Discounts** in the left sidebar.
