SSL certificates encrypt communications between a server and website visitors, protecting passwords, private data, and financial information from interception.

## Domain validated certificate (DV)

A DV certificate is tied directly to a domain name, assuring visitors they are on the correct site — commonly used to secure payment pages and other areas where customers share sensitive data. DV certificates are issued within five minutes on average; domains using Internationalized Domain Name (IDN) format can take up to three working days.

We provide:

* RapidSSL
* Comodo Positive SSL
* Comodo Essential SSL
* Thawte SSL123
* Comodo Essential SSL Wildcard
* RapidSSL Wildcard

A Wildcard certificate secures all subdomains of one domain with a single certificate; a standard certificate covers one specific domain only.

## Company validated certificate (OV)

An OV certificate requires additional documentation to verify the company's identity, unlike a DV certificate which only validates domain ownership. Browsers display verified company information alongside the certificate, giving visitors greater confidence in the site's authenticity.
