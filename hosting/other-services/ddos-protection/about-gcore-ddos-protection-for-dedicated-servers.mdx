## How it works

DDoS Protection is a service that protects Dedicated Servers from DDoS attacks.

We enable Basic (free) Protection on all our equipment by default. When a server is under attack, the system blocks the attacker's IP address, preventing further attacks. The server is undamaged but becomes unavailable for several hours.

You can order advanced (paid) Protection. The service routes all traffic through the TMS (threat mitigation system — a system for cleaning traffic and detecting threats) at all times. The device instantly detects attacks, cleans the traffic, and sends only legitimate data to the server. You can configure the TMS ACL (access control list). The main advantage is that the system does not block IP addresses during an attack, so the server remains available.

## Always-on protection

Advanced protection uses an always-on mode: your traffic passes through the TMS at all times, even when there is no attack. The response to any attack is instant, and the server is guaranteed to receive only clean traffic.

## Billing

The price of the DDoS Protection service depends on three things:

- OSI layers to be protected. Two ranges are available: L3-L4 and L3-L7; the first option is cheaper.
- The bandwidth that will be used by TMS to send traffic to the server. Several options are available: 1 Mbps, 10 Mbps, 100 Mbps, 200 Mbps, 500 Mbps, 1 Gbps, 2 Gbps, 10 Gbps. The lower the bandwidth, the cheaper the tariff.
- The region where the server is located. Prices vary by location. Please submit a service request, and we will provide pricing for DDoS Protection at a specific data center. 

We are also considering providing a tariff with individual conditions. Please describe the protection parameters you are interested in, and we will be happy to discuss an individual offer with you.
