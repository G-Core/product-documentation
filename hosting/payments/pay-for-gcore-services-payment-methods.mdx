title: Pay for Gcore services (payment methods)
sidebarTitle: Pay for Gcore services
ai-navigation: Configure payments in the Gcore hosting portal via PayPal, bank transfer, bank card, or Alipay using the Add funds wizard.
---

Payments for Gcore services are processed in USD or euro, depending on the account provider. Gcore accounts pay in euro; G-Core USD accounts pay in USD. To view the account provider, navigate to **Home** and check the **Information about account** section.

We support payments via PayPal, bank transfer, and bank cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Union Pay) and Alipay.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="PayPal">

<Note>PayPal charges a commission of 0.35 EUR + 3.41% of the payment amount.</Note>

1\. On the **Home** page, click **Add funds**.

<Frame>![Home page with Information about account section and Add funds button](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image1.png)</Frame>

2\. For **Step 1** (Select a payment method):

  * Enter the amount and select the currency.
  * Find **PayPal** and click **Pay**.

<Frame>![Step 1: Select a payment method with PayPal, Bank transfer, and Adyen listed](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image2.png)</Frame>

3\. For **Step 2** (Select a payer):

  * From the **Payer** dropdown, select an existing payer or choose **Add new**.
  * Select the **Country**.
  * Select the **Payer status**: Individual or Company.
  * Click **Next >>**.

<Frame>![Step 2: Select a payer with Payer, Country, and Payer status fields](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image3.png)</Frame>

4\. For **Step 3** (Payer information):

  * The **Payer status** field is pre-filled from Step 2.
  * Enter the **Contact person** name.
  * Enter the **Email** address.
  * Under **Actual address**, select the **Country**.
  * Click **Next >>**.

<Frame>![Step 3: Payer information with Contact person, Email, and Country fields](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image4.png)</Frame>

5\. For **Step 4** (Payment confirmation), confirm the payment amount, including the PayPal commission, and click **Pay**.

<Frame>![Step 4: Payment confirmation showing total amount with Pay button](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image5.png)</Frame>

6\. Log in to the PayPal account, or select **Pay by Debit or Credit Card** to pay as a PayPal guest, and complete the payment.

The money will be added to the account balance within 72 hours. If this does not happen, [write to technical support](/hosting/contact-our-technical-support).

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bank transfer">

<Note>The minimum payment amount for bank transfers is 10.00 EUR.</Note>

1\. On the **Home** page, click **Add funds**.

<Frame>![Home page with Information about account section and Add funds button](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image1.png)</Frame>

2\. For **Step 1** (Select a payment method):

  * Enter the amount and select the currency.
  * Find **Bank transfer** and click **Pay**.

<Frame>![Step 1: Select a payment method with Bank transfer option](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image2.png)</Frame>

3\. For **Step 2** (Select a payer):

  * From the **Payer** dropdown, select an existing payer or choose **Add new**.
  * Select the **Country**.
  * Select the **Payer status**: Individual or Company.
  * Click **Next >>**.

<Frame>![Step 2: Select a payer with Payer, Country, and Payer status fields](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image3.png)</Frame>

4\. For **Step 3** (Payer information):

  * The **Payer status** field is pre-filled from Step 2.
  * Enter the **Contact person** name.
  * Enter the **Email** address.
  * Under **Actual address**, select the **Country**.
  * Click **Next >>**.

<Frame>![Step 3: Payer information with Contact person, Email, and Country fields](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image4.png)</Frame>

5\. For **Step 4** (Payment confirmation), confirm the payment amount and click **Ok**.

<Frame>![Step 4: Payment confirmation showing total amount with Ok button](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image5.png)</Frame>

The system will generate an invoice in a new tab. Right-click to save or print it.

The invoice is also available in the control panel: navigate to **Billing** > **Payments** and click the required payment.

<Frame>![Billing Payments list with Add button](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image6.png)</Frame>

Contact the accounting department or the bank to complete the payment. When contacting the bank, specify the full pfx/XXX number — for example, "pfx/111111".

The money will be added to the account balance within 5 business days. If this does not happen, write to technical support. Describe the issue and attach a transaction statement with a bank stamp.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bank card or Alipay">

<Note>We do not charge a commission for this payment method, but the bank may.</Note>

1\. On the **Home** page, click **Add funds**.

<Frame>![Home page with Information about account section and Add funds button](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image1.png)</Frame>

2\. For **Step 1** (Select a payment method):

  * Enter the amount and select the currency.
  * Find **PayPal, Bank Cards, AMEX and Alipay** and click **Pay**.

<Frame>![Step 1: Select a payment method with PayPal, Bank Cards, AMEX and Alipay option](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image2.png)</Frame>

3\. For **Step 2** (Select a payer):

  * From the **Payer** dropdown, select an existing payer or choose **Add new**.
  * Select the **Country**.
  * Select the **Payer status**: Individual or Company.
  * Click **Next >>**.

<Frame>![Step 2: Select a payer with Payer, Country, and Payer status fields](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image3.png)</Frame>

4\. For **Step 3** (Payer information):

  * The **Payer status** field is pre-filled from Step 2.
  * Enter the **Contact person** name.
  * Enter the **Email** address.
  * Under **Actual address**, select the **Country**.
  * Click **Next >>**.

<Frame>![Step 3: Payer information with Contact person, Email, and Country fields](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image4.png)</Frame>

5\. For **Step 4** (Payment confirmation), confirm the payment amount and click **Pay**.

<Frame>![Step 4: Payment confirmation showing total amount with Pay button](/images/docs/hosting/payments/pay-for-gcore-services-payment-methods/pay-image5.png)</Frame>

6\. In the next window, select the payment method and enter the required information. To pay with a bank card, click the bank card icon. To pay via Alipay, click the Alipay icon.

The money is typically added to the account balance within 5–10 minutes. In some cases, it can take up to 24 hours. If the balance does not update after 24 hours, write to technical support.

Bank card payments support auto payment, which recharges the account balance automatically when it falls below the cost of a service. [Set up auto payment](/hosting/payments/set-up-auto-payment) covers how to enable and configure it.

</Tabs>
