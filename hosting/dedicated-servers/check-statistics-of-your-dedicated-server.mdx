The Control Panel provides traffic statistics for dedicated servers, showing the volume of incoming and outgoing data over a configurable time period.

## Incoming and outgoing traffic

To view traffic statistics for a server:

1. In the Control Panel, navigate to **Dedicated servers**.

2. Select the server and click **Statistics** in the toolbar.

3. Select a period and click **OK**.

## Total traffic volume

The **Common Traffic** section displays server-by-server traffic totals for the selected period.
