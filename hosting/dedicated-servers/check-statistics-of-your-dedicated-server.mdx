The Control Panel provides two traffic statistics views for dedicated servers: daily incoming and outgoing traffic data, and monthly consumption against the included quota.

## Consumption by days

To view daily incoming and outgoing traffic:

1. In the Control Panel, navigate to **Dedicated servers**.

2. Select the server and click **Statistics** in the toolbar.

3. On the **Service usage statistics** page, select a period and click **Show report**.

## Overage by month

The **Overage by month** tab shows total data consumption against the monthly included quota, along with remaining bandwidth and any overage charges for the selected month.

To view the overage report, click **Overage by month** at the top of the **Service usage statistics** page.
