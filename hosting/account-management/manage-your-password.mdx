The [Gcore&nbsp;Hosting&nbsp;Portal](https://hosting.gcore.com/billmgr) provides two ways to manage the account password: changing it from the profile page while logged in, or recovering it by email if forgotten.

## Change a password

1. Log in to the Hosting Portal.
2. In the **Client** section of the sidebar, click **Profile**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/docs/hosting/account-management/manage-your-password/manage-your-password-image1.png" width="70%" alt="Client profile page with Password and Confirmation fields" />
</Frame>

3. In the **Password** field, enter the new password. It must be at least eight characters long and include at least one uppercase letter and one digit.
4. In the **Confirmation** field, re-enter the new password.
5. Click **Ok**.

## Recover a forgotten password

Forgotten passwords can be reset by email verification. Submit the account email on the [recovery&nbsp;page](https://hosting.gcore.com/billmgr?func=recovery) and click **Ok**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/docs/hosting/account-management/manage-your-password/manage-your-password-image2.png" width="70%" alt="Password recovery page with email input" />
</Frame>

A recovery email arrives from [billing@gcore.com](mailto:billing@gcore.com) with the subject **Password Recovery**. If it does not arrive within a few minutes, look in the spam folder. If the email is still missing, submit the request again on the [recovery&nbsp;page](https://hosting.gcore.com/billmgr?func=recovery).

After three failed login attempts, the account is temporarily locked. To unlock it, contact support via chat on [gcore.com](https://gcore.com).
