---
title: Install a Linux OS from your ISO image
sidebarTitle: Install Linux from your ISO image
---

You can install an OS on your VPS for free. Exception: Windows. You can only buy Windows while ordering a server.

1\. Power down the virtual machine.


<Frame>![Power down the virtual machine](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/2017-10-04_20-18-26.png)</Frame>



2\. Go to "Disks".


<Frame>![Disks](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/2017-10-04_20-19-43.png)</Frame>



3\. Click **ISO**.


<Frame>![ISO](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/2017-10-04_20-20-21.png)</Frame>



4\. For the **Image name** menu, select your ISO. For the **Boot priority**, select **To the beginning** and click **OK.**


<Frame>![Image ](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/2017-10-04_20-21-16.png)</Frame>



5\. Make sure the disk image has appeared on the **Virtual disks** list.


<Frame>![Virtual disks](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/2017-10-04_20-21-44.png)</Frame>



6\. Restart your VM.


<Frame>![Restart your VM](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/2017-10-04_20-22-30.png)</Frame>



7\. Click **VNC**.


<Frame>![VNC](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/______________2017-08-28___3.56.20.png)</Frame>



You'll see the boot menu of your ISO.
