---

You can install an OS on your VPS for free. Exception: Windows. You can only buy Windows while ordering a server.

1\. Power down the virtual machine.


<Frame>![Power down the virtual machine](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image-image1.png)</Frame>



2\. Go to "Disks".


<Frame>![Disks](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image-image2.png)</Frame>



3\. Click **ISO**.


<Frame>![ISO](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image-image3.png)</Frame>



4\. For the **Image name** menu, select your ISO. For the **Boot priority**, select **To the beginning** and click **OK.**


<Frame>![Image ](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image-image4.png)</Frame>



5\. Make sure the disk image has appeared on the **Virtual disks** list.


<Frame>![Virtual disks](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image-image5.png)</Frame>



6\. Restart your VM.


<Frame>![Restart your VM](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image-image6.png)</Frame>



7\. Click **VNC**.


<Frame>![VNC](/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/manage/operating-system/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image/install-a-linux-os-from-your-iso-image-image7.png)</Frame>



You'll see the boot menu of your ISO.
