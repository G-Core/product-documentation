An IP address may be added to email blocklists if it was previously used to send spam or bulk messages. When an IP address is blocklisted, email providers may reject or filter outgoing messages.

<Note>
Blocklists are managed by independent organizations. Gcore cannot remove IP addresses from third-party blocklists and does not permit use of its services for distributing spam, chain letters, or unsolicited bulk email per clause 6.4 of the [User&nbsp;Agreement](https://gcore.com/legal) and the Acceptable Use Policy. If an IP is blocklisted, ordering an [additional&nbsp;IP](/hosting/dedicated-servers/manage/networking/additional-ip-addresses/buy-an-additional-ip-address) may restore email delivery.
</Note>

## Reduce the risk of blocklisting

Best practices for sending email from a Gcore server:

- Send mail only to recipients who opted in.
- Include an unsubscribe link in every email.
- Configure SPF, DKIM, and PTR records correctly.

## Check if the IP is in a blocklist

[MXToolbox](https://mxtoolbox.com/blacklists.aspx) checks a mail server IP against multiple email blocklists.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open MXToolbox">
    Open [MXToolbox](https://mxtoolbox.com/blacklists.aspx).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a blocklist check">
    Enter the server IP address in the **Server IP or Domain** field and click **Blacklist Check**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/troubleshooting/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists-image1.png" alt="MXToolbox blocklist check results showing LISTED entries" width="70%" />
    </Frame>

    The tool checks the IP against multiple blocklists and shows a **LISTED** or **OK** status for each.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the blocklist details">
    For each **LISTED** entry, click **Detail** to see the reason for listing and instructions for removal.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/troubleshooting/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists-image2.png" alt="LISTED entries with the Detail button highlighted" width="70%" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request delisting">
    Follow the removal instructions provided by the blocklist operator. Most blocklists require submitting a delisting request through a web form or email.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/troubleshooting/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists-image3.png" alt="Blocklist detail page showing the reason for listing" width="70%" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a response">
    Re-run the MXToolbox check periodically until the IP is no longer listed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
