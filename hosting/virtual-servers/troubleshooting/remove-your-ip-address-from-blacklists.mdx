An IP address may be added to email blocklists if it was previously used to send spam or bulk messages. When a mail server IP is blocklisted, some outgoing emails may not be delivered.

<Note>
Per clause 6.4 of the [User&nbsp;Agreement](https://gcore.com/legal) and the Acceptable Use Policy, Gcore cannot remove addresses from third-party blocklists and does not permit use of its services for distributing spam, chain letters, or unsolicited bulk email. If an IP is blocked, ordering an [additional&nbsp;IP](/hosting/dedicated-servers/manage/networking/additional-ip-addresses/buy-an-additional-ip-address) may help restore delivery.
</Note>

## Avoid being blocklisted

To reduce the risk of blocklisting when using Gcore services for email:

1. Include an unsubscribe link in every email.
2. Send only to recipients who have opted in to receive messages.
3. Verify that all DNS records are configured correctly.

## Check if the IP is in a blocklist

[MXToolbox](https://mxtoolbox.com/blacklists.aspx) checks a mail server IP against multiple email blocklists.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open MXToolbox">
    Open [MXToolbox](https://mxtoolbox.com/blacklists.aspx).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a blocklist check">
    Enter the server IP address in the **Server IP or Domain** field and click **Blacklist Check**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/troubleshooting/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists-image1.png" alt="MXToolbox blocklist check results showing LISTED entries" width="70%" />
    </Frame>

    The tool checks the IP against multiple blocklists and shows a **LISTED** or **OK** status for each.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the blocklist details">
    For each **LISTED** entry, click **Detail** to see the reason for listing and instructions for removal.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/troubleshooting/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists-image2.png" alt="LISTED entries with the Detail button highlighted" width="70%" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request delisting">
    Contact the blocklist operator and provide details about the reason for listing and the date Gcore services started being used. Submit the delisting request through the form or email address shown on the detail page.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/hosting/virtual-servers/troubleshooting/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists/remove-your-ip-address-from-blacklists-image3.png" alt="Blocklist detail page showing the reason for listing" width="70%" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a response">
    Delisting timelines vary by blocklist operator. Monitor the IP status in MXToolbox after submitting the request.
  </Step>
</Steps>
