---
title: "Bandwidth and traffic"
---

Bandwidth defines the maximum available network speed for compute resources in Gcore Edge Cloud.\
\
For Basic Virtual Machines, bandwidth is up to 200 Mbps.\
For Dedicated Virtual Instances, bandwidth is up to 1 Gbps. Actual throughput may exceed this value depending on network conditions.\
\
Bare Metal servers provide network configurations with speeds of 10 Gbps or higher, depending on the selected setup.\
\
Network traffic for cloud compute (Virtual Machines) is:

- Unlimited traffic
- Free incoming (ingress) and outgoing (egress) traffic

Gcore does not apply artificial throttling. The term “up to” indicates the maximum achievable bandwidth, and actual performance may vary depending on workload and network conditions.

For workloads that require high, consistent throughput, use Bare Metal servers with dedicated, bonded network interfaces (10 Gbps or higher).
