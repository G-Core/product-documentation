---
title: "Bandwidth and Traffic"
---

Bandwidth defines the maximum available network speed for compute resources in Gcore Edge Cloud.

For **Basic Virtual Machines**, bandwidth is up to 200 Mbps.\
For **Dedicated Virtual Instances**, bandwidth is up to 1 Gbps. Actual throughput may exceed this value depending on network conditions.

For workloads that require higher throughput, **Bare Metal servers** offer network configurations with speeds of 10 Gbps or higher, depending on the selected setup.

Network traffic for **cloud compute (Virtual Machines)** is:

- Unlimited
- Free of charge for both incoming (ingress) and outgoing (egress) traffic

Gcore does not apply artificial throttling. Bandwidth values use “up to” to indicate the maximum achievable speed and may vary depending on workload and network conditions.

Use Bare Metal servers with dedicated bonded network interfaces for workloads that require high, consistent throughput (10 Gbps or higher).
