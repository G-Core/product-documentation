All clients are subscribed to the PAYG (pay-as-you-go) billing plan. Edge Cloud resources are charged per minute for the time they are in use.

<Warning>
  To start using Edge Cloud services, add at least €4/$4 to the account balance. To pay monthly, [support team](https://gcore.com/contact-us).
</Warning>

Consumption for Cloud PAYG tariffs is collected approximately every five minutes. When accrued expenses reach the 4 EUR/USD threshold, the system deducts this amount from the existing balance. If the balance has insufficient funds, the system charges the amount from the added payment method. After a successful payment, resource usage continues until expenses reach the 4 EUR/USD threshold again.

If payment fails from both the Gcore balance and the added payment method, Edge Cloud services are suspended. Retention periods after suspension vary by product — for example, Virtual Machines, Volumes, and Bare Metal servers are deleted after 48 hours, while Managed Kubernetes, Function as a Service, and Container as a Service are deleted immediately. See [Data deletion](/account-settings/billing/data-deletion) for the full table of retention periods.

To avoid service suspension and maintain uninterrupted access to Cloud resources, top up the account balance in advance.

Up-to-date prices for Edge Cloud resources are available on the [pricing page](https://gcore.com/pricing/cloud).

Billing rules differ by resource type:

**Virtual Machines** are charged from the moment of creation until they are completely stopped. Volumes, snapshots, IP addresses (floating IP, virtual IP, and reserved IP address), images, and the Windows license are charged separately. Outgoing traffic is free.

**Volumes, IP addresses (floating IP, virtual IP, and reserved IP address), images, and snapshots** are charged from the moment of creation until deletion, regardless of whether the Virtual Machine is running.

**Bare Metal servers and AI IPUs and GPUs** are charged from the moment of creation until deletion, regardless of whether the server is running. IP addresses (floating IP, virtual IP, and reserved IP address), images, the Windows license, and outgoing traffic for Bare Metal are charged separately. For AI GPU and inference billing details, see [Edge AI](/edge-ai/billing).

**Managed Logging** is charged based on the volume of stored logs.

**Function as a Service** is charged for the compute resources used to execute the function.
