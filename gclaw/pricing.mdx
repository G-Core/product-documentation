Gclaw is a paid service currently under active development. Features, pricing, and limits may change upon reasonable prior notice.

## Plans

Two plans are available. Both include managed inference (no API keys required), Telegram and Discord integrations, and extra skills via Clawhub.

| Plan | Monthly price | Included tokens |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Lite | USD 10 / EUR 9 | 30M input + 6M output |
| Standard | USD 30 / EUR 26 | 100M input + 20M output |

Additional usage beyond the included bundle is billed at the same rate for both plans:

| | Rate |
|--|------|
| Input tokens | USD 0.11 / EUR 0.10 per 1M |
| Output tokens | USD 0.21 / EUR 0.20 per 1M |

## Trial

A 7-day trial is available once per account, regardless of plan. The trial includes 8M input tokens and 2M output tokens. Activating the trial requires adding a payment method; cancelling before the trial ends incurs no charge.

## Beta conditions

During the beta period:

- Each account is limited to one Gclaw instance (one VM).
- Instances operate under a spot tariff: they may be suspended or terminated — including while in use — on at least 24 hours' prior notice.
- No service level agreements, guarantees, or support commitments apply.
