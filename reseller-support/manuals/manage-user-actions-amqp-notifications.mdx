## What are User Actions AMQP notifications

User Actions Advanced Message Queuing Protocol (AMQP) notifications let you subscribe to events triggered by your customers and sellers — such as creating or deleting resources — and have those events delivered automatically to your own messaging infrastructure via an AMQP connection string.

You manage subscriptions from the Reseller Admin portal at <a href="https://admin.gcore.top" target="_blank">admin.gcore.top</a>.

## Subscribe to AMQP notifications

1. Go to <a href="https://admin.gcore.top" target="_blank">https://admin.gcore.top</a>.
2. Open **[SECTION NAME]** in the **[MENU NAME]** section. <!-- TODO: confirm nav path in portal -->
3. Click **Add connection string**. <!-- TODO: confirm button label -->
4. Enter your AMQP connection string in the field provided.
5. Click **Save**. <!-- TODO: confirm button label -->

## Send a test message

You can verify your connection string is working by sending a test message before relying on it for live events.

1. Go to <a href="https://admin.gcore.top" target="_blank">https://admin.gcore.top</a>.
2. Open **[SECTION NAME]** in the **[MENU NAME]** section. <!-- TODO: confirm nav path in portal -->
3. Click **Send test message** next to the subscription. <!-- TODO: confirm button label -->

## Unsubscribe from AMQP notifications

1. Go to <a href="https://admin.gcore.top" target="_blank">https://admin.gcore.top</a>.
2. Open **[SECTION NAME]** in the **[MENU NAME]** section. <!-- TODO: confirm nav path in portal -->
3. Find the subscription you want to remove and click **Unsubscribe**. <!-- TODO: confirm button label -->
4. Confirm the action.

Your endpoint will stop receiving event notifications.
