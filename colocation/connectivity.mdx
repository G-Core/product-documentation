Gcore offers connectivity services for Colocation customers who need internet access or private networking between sites. Each service is billed separately from rack space and power, and availability varies by location.

## Direct Internet Access

Direct Internet Access provides internet connectivity without requiring the customer to have their own Autonomous System Number (ASN): Gcore assigns the IP addresses and handles the routing. It's the most common connectivity add-on for new colocation customers.

## Billing model

That service is billed on a committed data rate, with traffic measured using the 95th percentile method and port usage charged separately based on port speed.

## Cross-connects

For customers who need a direct connection to a carrier, internet exchange, or another tenant in the same facility, a cross-connect provides a dedicated physical cable between the two parties. It's billed as a one-time installation fee plus a monthly charge.

## Leased lines

Where a cross-connect links parties in the same building, a leased line extends that connection between two separate locations with a guaranteed fixed bandwidth. It's billed monthly based on bandwidth, distance, and service type.

## Availability and ordering

Availability varies by location for all of these services. Contact the [sales team](https://gcore.com/contact-sales) to confirm what's offered at the specific facility and to place an order.
