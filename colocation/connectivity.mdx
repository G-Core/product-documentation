Gcore offers connectivity services for Colocation customers who need internet access or private networking between sites. Each service is billed separately from rack space and power, and availability varies by location.

## Direct Internet Access

Direct Internet Access provides internet connectivity without requiring the customer to have their own Autonomous System Number (ASN): Gcore assigns the IP addresses and handles the routing. It's the most common connectivity add-on for new colocation customers.

## Billing model

Direct Internet Access is billed on a committed data rate, with traffic measured using the 95th percentile method and port usage charged separately based on port speed.

## Cross-connects

A cross-connect is a dedicated physical cable connection between two parties in the same data center facility, billed as a one-time installation fee plus a monthly charge.

## Leased lines

A leased line is a dedicated point-to-point connection between two locations that guarantees a fixed bandwidth, billed monthly based on bandwidth, distance, and service type.
