Gcore offers connectivity services for Colocation customers who need internet access or private networking between sites. Each service is billed separately from rack space and power, and availability varies by location.

## Direct Internet Access

Direct Internet Access provides internet connectivity without requiring the customer to have their own Autonomous System Number (ASN). Gcore assigns the IP addresses and handles routing, making it suited for customers who need straightforward internet access. It's commonly ordered together with colocation.

## Billing model

Direct Internet Access is billed on a committed data rate, with traffic measured using the 95th percentile method. Port usage is charged separately based on port speed.

## Cross-connects

A cross-connect is a dedicated physical cable connection between two parties within the same data center facility. It's billed as a one-time installation fee plus a monthly charge.

## Leased lines

A leased line is a dedicated point-to-point connection between two locations with guaranteed bandwidth. It's billed monthly based on bandwidth, distance, and service type.
