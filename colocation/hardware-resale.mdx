Gcore can source and deliver hardware directly to a colocation data center on the customer's behalf. This covers servers, network devices, and smaller components such as transceivers, cables, and optics. Customers retain full ownership of all purchased hardware.

## What's included

The hardware resale service can include:

- Procurement of new or used hardware from major vendors
- Global logistics and delivery to the data center
- On-site installation coordinated with [Smart Hands](/colocation/smart-hands)
- Small parts and components that Gcore typically keeps in stock

Gcore's supply chain often provides more competitive pricing than purchasing hardware independently.

## Order hardware

To start the process, contact the [sales team](https://gcore.com/contact-sales) with the following details:

- Data center location
- Type and quantity of hardware required
- Preferred vendors, if any
- Required delivery date

Full payment is required before Gcore places the order with the vendor.
