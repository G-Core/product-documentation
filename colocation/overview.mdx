Gcore Colocation places customer-owned servers and network hardware in enterprise-grade data centers. It combines the reliability of Tier 3 and Tier 4 certified facilities with direct access to Gcore's global network and built-in DDoS protection, with 24/7 on-site technical support included in every plan.

## Rack options

Three configurations are available. Rack unit and full rack options can be ordered directly. Private cages are available on request for large enterprise deployments.

| Option | Space | Committed power |
|--------|-------|-----------------|
| Rack unit | 1U to 10U in a shared rack | 500 W per 1U |
| Full rack | 1 dedicated rack | 5 kW |
| Private cage | Multiple racks in a custom enclosed space | Custom |

Customers with rack units in a shared rack do not have direct physical access to their equipment. Gcore staff or authorized contractors perform all hardware operations on their behalf. Customers with full racks or private cages may arrange physical access to their equipment.

For current pricing on rack unit and full rack options, see [gcore.com/colocation](https://gcore.com/colocation). Private cage pricing is determined per deployment.

## Included services

Every plan includes the following services regardless of rack type or location.

### Power and cooling

Each rack includes redundant power at the committed capacity and active cooling managed by the data center facility. Customers who exceed their committed power allocation are charged an overcommit fee.

### Network connectivity

Colocated equipment connects directly to Gcore's global low-latency network, built for content delivery and high-performance applications. High-bandwidth internet access is part of the plan at every location. Additional connectivity services — IP Transit, IP Access, Cross-Connects, and Leased Lines — are available as [separate add-ons](/colocation/connectivity).

### DDoS protection

Traffic to colocated servers can be routed through Gcore's own scrubbing infrastructure before reaching the equipment. It's a native network capability, not a third-party service, and it's included at no additional cost.

### On-site technical support

Technical staff are on-site 24/7 to handle remote hands, hardware troubleshooting, racking and stacking, and emergency response. The [Smart Hands](/colocation/smart-hands) service is available as a paid add-on for more complex work: hardware installation, component replacement, diagnostics, and cable management.

## Data center tiers

Gcore colocates only in the same Tier 3 and Tier 4 facilities used for its own mission-critical infrastructure. Both tiers are certified under the Uptime Institute standard.

| Tier | Uptime | Redundancy model |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Tier 3 | 99.982% | N+1 |
| Tier 4 | 99.995% | 2N fully fault-tolerant |

Tier 3 facilities provide concurrent maintainability, meaning hardware can be serviced without taking the system offline. Tier 4 adds full fault tolerance, meaning any single component failure does not interrupt operations.
