Smart Hands is an on-site technical support service for Gcore Colocation customers. Data center engineers perform physical tasks directly on customer hardware — installing or removing equipment, replacing components, cabling, and any other hands-on work that requires physical access inside the facility.

## Use cases

Smart Hands applies when customer hardware is housed in a Gcore colocation facility and on-site intervention is needed. Two common scenarios:

- A hardware failure occurs but the customer's own team cannot travel to the data center to resolve it.
- Equipment installation, reconfiguration, or an urgent hardware task needs to be completed on-site.

## Priority tiers

Requests are handled at one of two priority levels, which affect both response time and billing rate.

### Standard priority

Standard priority covers requests that can be completed during normal business hours on a best-effort basis. It's suited for non-urgent tasks such as scheduled equipment installation, cable management, or routine hardware checks.

### High priority

High priority applies to requests that require an accelerated response or must be handled outside normal business hours. An engineer starts working on the next business day. This tier also covers any request explicitly marked as urgent.

## Billing

Smart Hands time is billed per hour. Time spent is rounded up to the nearest 15 minutes, and standard and high-priority work are billed at different rates.

As an example: two tasks at the same location taking 5 and 30 minutes produce a combined 35 minutes, which rounds up to 45 minutes for billing. Rates vary by location — contact the Gcore support team for current pricing at a specific facility.

## SLAs

| Priority | Response commitment |
|----------|-------------------|
| Standard | Best effort during business hours |
| High | Engineer starts next business day |

## Smart Hands requests

Submit a request through Gcore support and specify the priority level along with a description of the work needed. If no priority is specified, support assigns one based on the nature and urgency of the request.
