Smart Hands is a paid on-site technical support service for Gcore Colocation customers. Data center engineers perform physical tasks directly on customer hardware inside the facility. It's available when on-site intervention is needed but no team is on-site.

## Covered tasks

Smart Hands engineers perform physical tasks that require hands-on access to customer hardware at the data center. Work covered includes:

- Hardware installation and removal (servers, switches, drives, and other components)
- Component replacement (failed or degraded parts)
- Cabling and cable management
- Server reboots and power cycling
- Indicator light and status checks
- Visual inspection and basic diagnostics
- Any other physical task at the rack that requires on-site presence

Smart Hands does not cover work that requires customer credentials or software access to the equipment.

## Priority tiers

Four priority levels are available, grouped into two billing tiers. Priority is specified when submitting the request.

| Priority | Scope | Response commitment |
|----------|-------|---------------------|
| Low | Non-urgent work during business hours | Best effort, usually 1–2 business days |
| Medium | Non-urgent work during business hours | Best effort, usually 1–2 business days |
| High | Urgent work or work outside business hours | Best effort, usually same or next day |
| Urgent | Urgent work or work outside business hours | Best effort, usually same or next day |

Low and Medium are billed at the standard rate. High and Urgent are billed at a higher rate. If no priority is specified, the support team assigns one based on the request details.

## Billing

Smart Hands time is billed in 15-minute increments, rounded up across all requests in a calendar month. Low and Medium priority work are billed at one rate; High and Urgent are billed at a higher rate. Time is calculated separately per data center location and per priority tier.

For example: two tasks at the same location taking five and 30 minutes produce a combined 35 minutes, which rounds up to 45 minutes billed for that location. If one of those tasks was submitted as High priority, the High-priority time is calculated separately from the Low/Medium time.

Rates vary by location. Contact the [sales team](https://gcore.com/contact-sales) for pricing at the specific facility.

## Submit a request

Every Smart Hands request is submitted as a [service request](/colocation/service-requests), using the same channels and priority levels described there.
