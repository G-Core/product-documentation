Smart Hands is an on-site technical support service for Gcore Colocation customers. Data center engineers perform physical tasks directly on customer hardware: installing or removing equipment, replacing components, cabling, and any other hands-on work that requires physical access inside the facility.

## Use cases

Smart Hands applies when customer hardware is housed in a Gcore colocation facility and on-site intervention is needed. Two common scenarios:

- A hardware failure occurs but the customer's own team cannot travel to the data center to resolve it.
- Equipment installation, reconfiguration, or an urgent hardware task needs to be completed on-site.

## Priority tiers

Two priority levels are available, each with a different response commitment and billing rate.

| Priority | Scope | Response commitment |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| Standard | Non-urgent work during business hours | Best effort during business hours |
| High | Urgent or out-of-hours work | Engineer starts next business day |

## Billing

Smart Hands time is billed per hour. Time spent is rounded up to the nearest 15 minutes, and standard and high-priority work are billed at different rates.

As an example: two tasks at the same location taking 5 and 30 minutes produce a combined 35 minutes, which rounds up to 45 minutes for billing. Rates vary by location.

Contact the [sales team](https://gcore.com/contact-sales) for current pricing at a specific facility.

## Smart Hands requests

Submit a request through [Gcore Support](mailto:support@gcore.com) and specify the priority level along with a description of the work needed. If no priority is specified, support assigns one based on the nature and urgency of the request.
