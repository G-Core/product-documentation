## Storage types and pricing

Gcore Object Storage offers three storage types:

### S3 Standard

General-purpose storage for most workloads.

| Item | Price (EUR) | Price (USD) |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Storage | €0.04/GB/month | $0.044/GB/month |
| Egress | €0.02/GB | $0.022/GB |
| Ingress | Free | Free |
| Requests | €0.03/10K | $0.033/10K |

**Self-commit plans:** Standard Storage offers discounted self-commit pricing plans for 5 TB and 20 TB commitments. Self-commit plans can be used in any S3 Standard location. For details, see the [pricing page](https://gcore.com/pricing/cloud).

### SFTP

SFTP storage has the same pricing and billing terms as S3 Standard.

### S3 Fast

High-performance storage optimized for AI/ML workloads, IoT telemetry, and latency-sensitive applications. Available in Sines-2, Sines-3, and London-2.

| Item | Price (EUR) | Price (USD) |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Storage | €0.06364/GiB/month | $0.07/GiB/month |
| Egress | €0.0013/GB | $0.00143/GB |
| Ingress | Free | Free |
| Requests | €0.03/10K | $0.033/10K |

<Note>
Self-commit plans are not currently available for S3 Fast storage. For committed volume discounts, contact the [sales team](https://gcore.com/contact-sales). Current pricing and plan details are available on the [pricing page](https://gcore.com/pricing/storage).
</Note>

## Billing rules

The plan subscription fee and the traffic overcommitment fee are charged on the 2nd day of each month. If the payment fails, the service is suspended.

Additional features are charged at the beginning of the month.

## Change a plan

To change a plan, navigate to the Profile icon in the lower-left corner of the page and select **Billing**.

The new plan is applied on the 1st day of the next month.

For questions, contact support via chat or email at [support@gcore.com](mailto:support@gcore.com).

## Object storage limits

- Maximum 1000 buckets per storage
- Maximum 10 million objects per bucket (for optimal performance, keep under 100K objects and distribute across multiple buckets)
