The CDN resource is the proxy between Gcore caching edge servers and your website's origin. You should create a CDN resource to enhance the download speed with our infrastructure.

## Choose your CDN resource type

There are three types of CDN resources available:

  * **For the entire site**. Integrate your entire website with our CDN and Managed DNS products. Best for complete site acceleration with automatic DNS management. Consult our guide on [configuring a CDN resource for the entire site](/cdn/getting-started/create-a-cdn-resource/create-a-cdn-resource-for-the-entire-site).

  * **For static assets only**. Integrate only static content delivery, such as images, CSS, JavaScript, and fonts. Best for websites where you want to accelerate only specific assets. Consult our guide on [configuring a CDN resource for static assets only](/cdn/getting-started/create-a-cdn-resource/create-a-cdn-resource-for-only-static-files).

  * **For video streaming**. Deliver live streams and video-on-demand content globally. Best for HLS, DASH, or FLV video delivery without transcoding. Consult our guide on [configuring a CDN resource for video streaming](/cdn/getting-started/create-a-cdn-resource/create-a-cdn-resource-for-video-streaming).

<Info>
Need transcoding? If you need to transcode raw video inputs (RTMP, SRT) or generate adaptive bitrate ladders automatically, use the [Video Streaming platform](/streaming) instead of Video CDN.
</Info>

## Quick comparison

| Feature | Entire Site | Static Assets | Video Streaming |
|---------|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Best for** | Complete site acceleration | Images, CSS, JS, fonts | Live streams & VOD |
| **DNS management** | Integrated | Manual | Manual |
| **Setup complexity** | Medium | Easy | Medium |
| **Typical use case** | E-commerce, blogs | Asset acceleration | Broadcasting, OTT |

**Note**: You can create up to thirty CDN resources of all types combined. If you need an increase to this limitation, contact your personal manager or [Gcore Support](mailto:support@gcore.com).
