# Getting started with Gclaw

This guide walks you through launching your first Gclaw instance and getting started with your personal AI assistant.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have a Gcore account. If you do not have one, [sign up for free](https://auth.gcore.com/login/signup).

## Launch your instance

To create and launch your Gclaw instance:

1. In the Gcore Customer Portal, navigate to **AI** > **Gclaw**.

2. If this is your first time accessing Gclaw, review the Terms of Use and click **I Agree**.

3. Click **Get started for free**.

4. Wait approximately one minute for your instance to be prepared.

5. Once ready, you will see a welcome message from Gclaw and the chat interface will become active.

## Explore the interface

After your instance is ready, you will see the Gclaw chat interface with the following elements:

- **Chat area**: Displays the conversation between you and your AI assistant. The welcome message provides tips for getting started.

- **Message input**: Type your messages and questions at the bottom of the screen. Press Enter or click the Send button to submit.

- **Settings button**: Click the gear icon in the top-right corner to access instance management options such as Restart Instance, Backup Config, Restore Config, and Recreate Instance.

## Start chatting

Your Gclaw assistant can help you with various tasks. The welcome message includes these tips:

- **Settings and Backups**: Click the gear icon to find Backup Config and Restore Config buttons. Back up before making big changes so you can roll back if something breaks.

- **Setting Up Connections**: Ask your assistant for copy-paste config snippets to connect messaging platforms like Telegram, Discord, or WhatsApp.

- **Enable Web Search**: Add your [Brave Search API key](https://brave.com/search/api/) to enable web search capabilities. Ask your assistant "Help me enable web search" for instructions.

- **About Memory**: Gclaw starts fresh each session and does not remember previous conversations by default. The assistant keeps notes in the workspace to build continuity over time.

## Ask for help

You can ask your assistant questions directly in the chat. For example:

- "How do I connect Telegram?"
- "How do I connect Discord?"
- "Help me enable web search"
- "How do I back up my configuration?"

Your assistant will provide step-by-step instructions with copy-paste commands.
