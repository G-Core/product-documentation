# Manage your Gclaw instance

This guide explains how to manage your Gclaw instance, including backing up your configuration, restoring from a backup, restarting, and recreating your instance.

## Access instance settings

To access the instance management options:

1. Open Gclaw in the Gcore Customer Portal.

2. Click the **Settings** button (gear icon) in the top-right corner of the chat interface.

3. The Settings panel displays the following options:
   - Restart Instance
   - Backup Config
   - Restore Config
   - Recreate Instance

## Backup your configuration

Creating regular backups of your configuration helps you recover from misconfigurations or unwanted changes.

<Tip>
Always create a backup before making significant changes to your instance, such as adding new skills, connections, or modifying settings.
</Tip>

To back up your configuration:

1. Click **Settings** (gear icon).

2. Click **Backup Config**.

3. Your current configuration is saved. The backup includes:
   - Channel configurations (Telegram, Discord, etc.)
   - Skills and integrations
   - Custom settings

## Restore your configuration

If something goes wrong after making changes, you can restore your previous configuration.

To restore from a backup:

1. Click **Settings** (gear icon).

2. Click **Restore Config**.

3. Your instance will revert to the most recent backup.

<Warning>
Restoring your configuration will overwrite your current settings. Any changes made since the last backup will be lost.
</Warning>

## Restart your instance

Restarting your instance can help resolve temporary issues or apply configuration changes that require a restart.

To restart your instance:

1. Click **Settings** (gear icon).

2. Click **Restart Instance**.

3. Wait for the instance to restart. This typically takes less than a minute.

### When to restart

Consider restarting your instance when:

- Configuration changes do not seem to take effect
- The assistant becomes unresponsive
- You experience unexpected behavior
- After applying updates

## Recreate your instance

Recreating your instance creates a fresh installation, removing all configurations and returning to the default state.

<Warning>
Recreating your instance will permanently delete all your configurations, including channel connections, skills, and custom settings. This action cannot be undone. Create a backup before recreating if you want to preserve your settings.
</Warning>

To recreate your instance:

1. Click **Settings** (gear icon).

2. Click **Recreate Instance**.

3. Confirm the action when prompted.

4. Wait for the new instance to be created. This typically takes about one minute.

### When to recreate

Consider recreating your instance when:

- You want to start fresh with a clean configuration
- Your instance is in an unrecoverable state
- You are troubleshooting persistent issues
- You want to reset all settings to defaults

## Best practices

Follow these recommendations to manage your Gclaw instance effectively:

1. **Regular backups**: Create backups before making any significant changes.

2. **Test changes incrementally**: Make one change at a time and verify it works before making additional changes.

3. **Document your configuration**: Keep notes about your custom settings outside of Gclaw in case you need to recreate your instance.

4. **Use restart first**: If you experience issues, try restarting before recreating. Restart preserves your configuration while resolving many common issues.
