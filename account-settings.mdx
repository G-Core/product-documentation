title: Account settings

---

The account icon in the top-right corner of the [Gcore Customer Portal](https://portal.gcore.com) opens **Profile**, which leads to a left-side menu covering profile information, billing, user access, API tokens, activity auditing, and notification preferences.

  * **My profile** - username, email, language, two-factor authentication, password, and login history
  * **Account** - general account information, billing address, tax settings, payment methods, account deletion, and active services
  * **Billing** - expenses, payments, invoices, and bonuses and discounts
  * **All users** - user roles, permissions, and invitations
  * **API tokens** - token creation, roles, and expiration
  * **Audit log** - a record of account and API activity
  * **Notifications** - email preferences for account and service events
