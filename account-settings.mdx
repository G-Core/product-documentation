title: Account settings

Use the **Account settings** section to manage personal profile, account configuration, billing details, and user access. All options in this section apply to the currently selected account.

To open this section, click the profile icon in the top-right corner of the Customer Portal and select **Profile**.

<img src="/images/docs/account-settings/profile-menu.png" alt="Account menu in the Customer Portal" width="300"/>

## Overview

### Personal

- **My profile** — Username, email, language, two-factor authentication, password, and login history

### Account and billing

- **Account** — Account ID and type, billing address, tax configuration, payment methods, and services management
- **Billing** — Expenses breakdown, payment history, bonus credits, and discounts

### Access and security

- **All users** — Invite users, assign roles, and manage permissions
- **API tokens** — Token management for programmatic access to Gcore services
- **Audit log** — Activity log showing actions performed on the account
- **Notifications** — Email notification preferences organized by service
